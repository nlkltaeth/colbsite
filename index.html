What is COLB?

COLB Asset SA is a Swiss fintech company pioneering the tokenization of real-world assets (RWA). Founded in 2020 in Geneva, we're making premium investment opportunities accessible to everyone through blockchain technology.

Traditional finance has locked away the best investments behind high barriers. We're changing that. Through our innovative TKSP Framework, you can now invest in:

  • • Swiss wealth management strategies
  • • Pre-IPO companies (SpaceX, Ramp, Varda)
  • • Premium investment funds
  • • Geo-strategic sectors (Energy, Infrastructure, Agribusiness)

TKSP Framework

Tokenized Structured Products (TKSP) are digital tokens that mirror the performance of real-world assets. Think of them as digital shares of premium investments, fully regulated and backed by Swiss law.

CMT

Colb Managed Tokens

Actively managed portfolios by professional wealth managers. Diversified across equities, bonds, and alternatives.

Risk: Medium | APY: ~7.5%

CTT

Colb Tracker Tokens

Track hard-to-reach assets and portfolios. Get exposure to exclusive pre-IPO companies and private markets.

Risk: High | Potential: 15%+

CFT

Colb Fund Tokens

Mirror the performance of specific funds like BlackRock. Access top-tier funds without minimum investment barriers.

Risk: Low-Medium | APY: ~6%

First TKSP Success: Our C1 Black token tracked the BlackRock World Technology Fund, sold out in 24 hours, and delivered +6.59% returns in 2023!
